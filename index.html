Myths and Facts

"You Can’t Swim on Your Period – Busted!"

Fact: It’s perfectly safe to swim during your period, especially if you use tampons or menstrual cups for protection.

"Eating Sour Foods Causes Period Pain – True or False?"

Fact: There’s no scientific evidence linking sour foods to increased menstrual pain. Period cramps are caused by the uterus contracting to shed its lining.

"Tampons Can Get Lost Inside You – Is It Possible?"

Fact: A tampon cannot get lost inside your body. It can only go as far as the end of the vaginal canal, which is blocked by the cervix.

"Periods Sync Up When Women Live Together – Myth or Magic?"

Fact: Menstrual synchrony is mostly a myth. Similar cycle timing among women is usually just random chance, not a biological effect.
