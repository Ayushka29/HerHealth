Menstrual Health Hygiene Tips

Basic Hygiene Tips

  • Change sanitary products regularly to prevent infections.
  • Wash your hands before and after changing sanitary products.
  • Wear comfortable, breathable underwear to prevent irritation.
  • Maintain a balanced diet to support overall health.
  • Stay hydrated and exercise regularly to manage symptoms.
  • Keep the vaginal area clean and dry to prevent bacterial growth.
